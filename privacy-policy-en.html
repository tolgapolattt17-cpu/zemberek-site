Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Data Collection

Zemberek collects the following data to manage your watch collection:

  • Watch information (brand, model, price, etc.)
  • Watch photos (stored locally on your device)
  • Apple ID for optional CloudKit synchronization

All collected data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties.

2. Data Usage

Your data is only used for the following purposes:

  • Viewing and managing your watch collection
  • Tracking your watch values
  • Optional synchronization between your devices

Your data is not used for advertising, analytics, or third-party services.

3. iCloud and CloudKit

Zemberek uses Apple's CloudKit service for iCloud synchronization.

  • Synchronization is completely optional
  • Your data is subject to Apple's privacy policy
  • Photos are synchronized via CloudKit
  • You can disable synchronization at any time

4. Camera and Gallery Access

The app requests the following permissions to add watch photos:

  • Camera: To take new watch photos
  • Photo Gallery: To select existing photos

These permissions are optional and not required for basic app functionality.

5. Data Security

  • All data is stored encrypted on your device
  • CloudKit synchronization is end-to-end encrypted
  • No data is transferred to third-party servers
  • Secure authentication with Sign in with Apple

6. Your Rights

  • You can delete your data at any time
  • You can close your account and clear all data
  • You can enable or disable CloudKit synchronization
  • You can manage permissions from iOS settings
